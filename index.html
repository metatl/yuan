Cloud–Aerosol Interactions

We investigate how aerosols modify cloud microphysics and radiative forcing using satellite retrievals (e.g., MODIS, CALIPSO) and reanalysis.

  • Microphysical pathways (CCN, droplet size, optical depth)
  • Radiative effects and cloud feedbacks
  • Process-level constraints from multi-sensor synergy

Submesoscale Dynamics

We study 0.1–10 km dynamics and their role in vertical exchange, productivity, and air–sea interaction using theory, high-res models, and observations.
