Atmospheric & Oceanic Research

Remote sensing · aerosols & clouds · climate dynamics

Research

We study geophysical fluid dynamics and the Earth system using theory, observations, and numerical models.

Cloud–Aerosol Interactions

How aerosols modify cloud microphysics and radiative forcing using satellite retrievals.

Submesoscale Dynamics

0.1–10 km dynamics and their role in exchange, productivity, and air–sea interaction.

Remote Sensing Methods

Multi-sensor fusion and uncertainty quantification for climate data records.
